Real definition :

A hacker is someone skilled in computer systems, networks, and software, often adept at identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities.

Their role depends on their intent :

  • Ethical hackers (White hats): Use their skills to improve security by testing systems for weaknesses, helping organizations defend against cyber threats.
  • Malicious hackers (Black hats): Exploit vulnerabilities for personal gain or to cause harm, such as stealing data or deploying malware.
  • Gray hats: Operate in the ethical gray area, often exposing vulnerabilities without permission but without malicious intent.

Hackers play a crucial role in the evolving cybersecurity landscape, pushing the boundaries of technology and security measures.
