Real definition :

A cybersecurity expert is a professional responsible for protecting computer systems, networks, and data from cyber threats.

Their role includes :

  • Threat prevention : Implementing tools and strategies to safeguard against hacking, malware, and phishing attacks.
  • Monitoring & detection : Continuously observing systems for suspicious activity and identifying vulnerabilities.
  • Incident response : Investigating breaches, mitigating damage, and restoring compromised systems.
  • Policy development : Creating security protocols, training staff, and ensuring compliance with regulations.

Cybersecurity experts are essential in safeguarding digital assets and ensuring the resilience of organizations against cyberattacks.
