Real definition :

A data analyst is a professional who collects, processes, and interprets data to help organizations make informed decisions.

  • Data collection : Gathering relevant data from various sources.
  • Data cleaning : Ensuring the data is accurate, complete, and free of errors.
  • Analysis : Using statistical methods, tools, and visualization techniques to uncover trends, patterns, and insights.
  • Reporting : Presenting findings through dashboards, reports, or visualizations to support decision-making.

Data analysts play a vital role in transforming raw data into actionable insights, driving business strategies and operational improvements.
