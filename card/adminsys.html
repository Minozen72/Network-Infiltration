Real definition :

A system administrator is responsible for maintaining, configuring, and ensuring the reliable operation of computer systems and networks.

Their role includes :

  • System maintenance : Installing, updating, troubleshooting operating systems, servers, and software.
  • Network management : Ensuring connectivity, configuring firewalls, and monitoring network performance.
  • Security : Implementing measures to protect systems from unauthorized access, malware, and data breaches.
  • User support : Assisting users with technical issues and ensuring smooth daily operations.
  • Backup & recovery : Managing data backups and ensuring systems can be restored after failures.

Sysadmins are essential for keeping IT infrastructure efficient, secure, and operational.
