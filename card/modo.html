Real definition :

A moderator is responsible for maintaining order and fostering a positive environment in online communities, forums, or events.

  • Content review : Monitoring posts, comments, and discussions to ensure they follow community guidelines.
  • Conflict resolution : Addressing disputes or inappropriate behavior to maintain a respectful atmosphere.
  • Enforcement : Removing harmful content and applying penalties when rules are violated.
  • Engagement : Encouraging meaningful interactions and guiding discussions to keep the community active and focused.

Moderators are essential for creating safe, constructive, and engaging spaces for participants.
